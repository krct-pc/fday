Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

You can register for any event by visiting the specific event page and clicking on the "Register Now" button. This will redirect you to a Google Form where you can complete your registration.

Are there any registration fees?

Registration fees vary depending on the event. Some events are free, while others may have a nominal fee. The registration fee details are mentioned on each event's page.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as there are no scheduling conflicts. Make sure to check the event dates and timings before registering.

How can I become a sponsor?

If you're interested in sponsoring our events, please contact us at sponsors@drifton.synerix.com with your proposal, and our team will get back to you with sponsorship packages and details.
