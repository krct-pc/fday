About Eventra

Eventra is a dynamic startup under the parent organization Programming Club 24, dedicated to creating engaging and innovative events for tech enthusiasts and creative minds alike. Our mission is to provide a platform for individuals to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and connect with like-minded people.

Whether you're a coding expert, a gaming enthusiast, or someone looking to explore new interests, Eventra has something exciting for everyone. Join us in our journey of exploration, innovation, and fun!
