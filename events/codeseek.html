Code Seek

Hunt. Code. Conquer.

September 15, 2023
10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Tech Hub, Main Campus

About the Event

Code Seek is an exciting coding treasure hunt that challenges participants to solve a series of programming puzzles and riddles. Each solution leads to the next clue, creating an engaging journey through various programming concepts and challenges.

Participants will need to demonstrate their problem-solving skills, coding abilities, and logical thinking to progress through the hunt. The event is designed to test not only your coding knowledge but also your ability to think outside the box.

Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting your coding journey, Code Seek offers challenges suitable for all skill levels. Join us for a day of coding adventure and fun!

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to 3 members.
  • All participants must bring their own laptops with necessary development environments set up.
  • Internet access will be provided, and participants are allowed to use online resources.
  • The event will consist of 5 rounds of increasing difficulty.
  • Each round has a time limit, and points will be awarded based on completion time and accuracy.
  • Use of pre-written code or solutions is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must follow the code of conduct and maintain academic integrity.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Prizes

Winners will receive exciting prizes and recognition:

  • 1st Place: $500 cash prize + Tech gadgets worth $300
  • 2nd Place: $300 cash prize + Tech gadgets worth $200
  • 3rd Place: $150 cash prize + Tech gadgets worth $100
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.
  • Special recognition for innovative solutions and approaches.
