Gaming Tournament

Battle for glory

September 16-17, 2023
10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Gaming Arena, Student Center

About the Event

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping gaming experience at Drifton's Gaming Tournament! This two-day event brings together gaming enthusiasts from all skill levels to compete in various popular game titles.

The tournament will feature multiple gaming categories including FPS (First Person Shooter), MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena), Racing, and Battle Royale. Players can showcase their skills, strategies, and reflexes as they compete for the championship title.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive player, this tournament offers a platform to connect with fellow gamers, make new friends, and enjoy the thrill of competition in a fun and engaging environment.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants can register for multiple game categories.
  • Each game category will have its own set of specific rules and tournament format.
  • Players must bring their own controllers/peripherals (keyboards, mice, etc.).
  • Gaming consoles and PCs will be provided at the venue.
  • All matches will be supervised by tournament officials.
  • Unsportsmanlike behavior will result in disqualification.
  • Participants must arrive 30 minutes before their scheduled match time.
  • The decision of the tournament officials will be final and binding.

Featured Games

  • FPS Category: Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Valorant
  • MOBA Category: League of Legends, Dota 2
  • Battle Royale: Fortnite, PUBG
  • Racing: Forza Horizon 5, Gran Turismo 7
  • Fighting: Street Fighter 6, Mortal Kombat 11
  • Sports: FIFA 23, NBA 2K23

Prizes

Exciting prizes await the winners in each category:

  • 1st Place (each category): Gaming gear worth $300 + Trophy
  • 2nd Place (each category): Gaming gear worth $150
  • 3rd Place (each category): Gaming gear worth $75
  • All participants will receive gaming merchandise.
  • Special award for the 'Most Valuable Player' across all categories.
